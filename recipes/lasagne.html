Description

This dish is lasagne. Need I say more?

Ingredients (courtesy of allrecipes)

  • 1 lb. Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb. lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste (wtf)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 12 lasagne noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb. mozzarella
  • 3/4 cup parmesan, grated

Steps:

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Cook meats.
  3. Stir in tomato ingredients.
  4. Bring a large pot of water to boil. Cook lasagne noodles
    in water for 8-10 minutes.
  5. Combine cheese and egg ingredients in bowl and add salt.
  6. Preheat oven to 375F.
  7. Assemble noodles, sauce, and cheese alternating.
  8. Repeat layers until ingredients done.
  9. Bake in oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, then bake for another 25.
  10. EAT. EAT NOW.
